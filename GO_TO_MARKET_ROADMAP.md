# AURELIUM

## From MVP to Living Software

---

# Strategic Principle

Do not sell the vision.

Sell the value.

Most startups fail because they try to build the final vision before proving the first layer of value.

AURELIUM should evolve through three stages:

```text
Stage 1 → Revenue
Stage 2 → Moat
Stage 3 → Revolution
```

---

# STAGE 1

# MVP VENDÁVEL

Timeline: Months 0-12

Goal:

Generate revenue.

At this stage AURELIUM is NOT a Living Software Fabric.

It is sold as:

"AI-Native Autonomous Engineering Platform"

# Phase 1

## Intent Core

Duration: 2 Months

### Problem Solved

Companies struggle translating business goals into technical work.

### Solution

Convert business objectives into executable missions.

Example:

```yaml
Goal:
  Reduce support costs
```

↓

```yaml
Missions:
  Automate FAQs
  Optimize workflows
  Reduce response time
```

### Revenue Model

SaaS Subscription

### Customers

* Startups
* Agencies
* SMBs
