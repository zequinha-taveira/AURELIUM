# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, professional, and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful
* Be constructive
* Be professional
* Focus on technical excellence
* Support collaboration

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment
* Discrimination
* Personal attacks
* Trolling
* Spam
* Disclosure of private information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, revoke permissions, or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.
